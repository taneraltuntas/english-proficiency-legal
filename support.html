Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases". Your premium subscription will be restored if you have an active subscription.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device to manage or cancel your subscription.

My progress was lost. Can I recover it?

Your progress is stored locally on your device. If you deleted and reinstalled the app, unfortunately the progress cannot be recovered. We recommend keeping the app installed to preserve your data.

How do I disable notifications?

You can disable notifications in your device's Settings > Notifications > English Proficiency.

The app is crashing. What should I do?

Please try the following steps:

  • Make sure you have the latest version of the app
  • Restart your device
  • If the issue persists, contact us with details about when the crash occurs
