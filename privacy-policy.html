Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

English Proficiency ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • University selection preference
  • Learning level preference (A2, B1, B2, C1)
  • App settings and preferences

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage statistics (screens visited, features used)
  • Practice session data (locally stored)
  • Crash reports and performance data

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

Firebase Analytics (Google)

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our app. This helps us improve the user experience. Firebase may collect:

  • App usage patterns
  • Device information
  • Approximate location (country level)

Learn more: Firebase Privacy Policy

Google AdMob

We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers
  • Ad interaction data

Learn more: Google Privacy Policy

RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect:

  • Purchase history
  • Subscription status
  • Anonymous user identifiers

Learn more: RevenueCat Privacy Policy

OneSignal

We use OneSignal to send push notifications. OneSignal may collect:

  • Device tokens for push notifications
  • Notification interaction data

Learn more: OneSignal Privacy Policy

Data Storage

Your practice progress, exam results, and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not store this data on our servers. If you delete the app, this data will be permanently removed.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with the third-party services mentioned above for the purposes described in this policy.

Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for users of all ages preparing for English proficiency exams. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored locally on your device
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings
  • Disable push notifications in your device settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
